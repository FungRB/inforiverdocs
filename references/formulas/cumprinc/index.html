CUMPRINC

The CUMPRINC function returns cumulative principal paid on a loan between two periods.

Syntax#

Example#

Consider a $10,000 loan at an annual rate of 10% that is to be paid off in 3 years. All payments are made at the beginning of the month.

To calculate the principal payment for a specified range of periods, say between period 12 and 18, the formula should be

The cumulative principal payments between 12th and 18th months is calculated to be $1882.04
