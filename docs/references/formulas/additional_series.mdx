## Syntax

`DSSERIES[seriesid]` - will represent the respective series data.

## Example

Consider the data in the following structure:

| Parameter | Series 1 | Series 2 | Series 3 | Series 4 |
|-----------|----------|----------|----------|----------|
| US        | 110      | 95       | 75       | 78       |
| India     | 85       | 70       | 100      | 80       |
| Germany   | 25       | 25       | 0        | 0        |

Here you can access the Series 3 value of India using the following formula:

```javascript
SUM(DSSERIES3.India)
```

The result will be: 100.
