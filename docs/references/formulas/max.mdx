The `MAX` function returns the largest number in a set of values. Cell references also accepted as arguments.

## Syntax

```javascript
MAX (value1,[value2]....)
```

## Arguments

`value1,value2 …` – The nodes or numbers of which you want the largest of.

## Example

| Account           | Q1    | Q2    | Q3    | Q4    |
|-------------------|-------|-------|-------|-------|
| Sales Expense     | 10.1m | 14.5m | 13.2m | 12.4m |
| Marketing Expense | 11.3m | 12.0m | 14.6m | 13.2m |
| <b>MAX</b>        | 11.3m | 14.5m | 14.6m | 13.2m |

To calculate the maximum of the two expenses in the resultant row 'MAX', the formula should be:

```javascript
MAX(Sales Expense, Marketing Expense)
```
